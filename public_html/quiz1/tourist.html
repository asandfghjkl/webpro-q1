T O U R I S T   P L A C E S

Beach and Tulungagung are inseparable things. The nature beauty of the sandy beaches on the southern coast can’t be overlooked, trully the gems of the city. There are plenty beaches you can visit here.

Nowadays, the popular ones are Kedung Tumpang beach. What makes Kedung Tumpang impressive is the rock cliffs swept by the swell and the natural small ponds. the clear turquoise water has enticed both locals and foreigners to take time off in Kedung Tumpang.

Another beaches we can visit in Tulungagung are Sanggar Beach, Sine Beach, Popoh Beach, Klatak Beach, Dlodo Beach, Sioro Beach, Gemah Beach, Blumbung Beach and many more.
Ranu Gumbolo Tulungagung, is a beautiful lake, famously known as Waduk Wonorejo by the locals. This charm is located about 24 km away from the city center. While exploring here, you are not only pampered by the nature beauty. The visitors could enjoy some facilities such as a play ground, fishing arena, and speedboat rental for the lake exploration.
Who says that Tulungagung is only identical with the beaches? Like the rests of the regions in East Java, the city of marble has modern theme waterparks. It’s where you can feel the great sensation of playing with water. There are Jambooland waterpark and Splash Waterpark.

In Jambooland, Aside of playing with water, the visitors are free to try the challenging ride like the rollercoaster – it’s great to trigger your adrenaline. And for Splash waterpark, the visitors are free to try a vast array of water rides ranging from waterslide, race slide, and spilling bucket. It also features the giant aquarium, food court, toilets, and a play ground so that the people feel comfortable and stay longer.
An attraction used to be known as Kusuma Wicitra Park is located in the heart of the city, on Jalan R.A. Kartini. The shady trees and beautiful garden add the charm of Aloon-Aloon Park. It opens 24hours a day and free, you just need to pay the parking service

Despite its limited area, it comes with complete facilities to ease the visitors such as a jogging track, an outbound venue, a couple of rides, a yard, and a skateboard arena. Additionally, the visitors could enjoy the complimentary WiFi, reflexology pool, fish pond, and clean toilet. For those seeking selfie spot, be sure to show your pose before the 30-meter-tall effigy of Garuda and the fountain with colorful lights.
